---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve dashboard login/access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to log in to `https://dashboard.aagnia.com`
- Errors: "Invalid credentials" or "Access denied"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login details">
    Ensure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" and follow reset instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cache/cookies and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If issues persist, contact support.
