## Overview

To start using Aagnia Technologies services:

1. Visit `https://aagnia.com` and click Sign Up
2. Complete registration with business details
3. Confirm your email address
4. Log in to the client dashboard

## Next Steps

Explore available solutions, manage projects, or contact our consulting team directly from your dashboard for personalized support.
