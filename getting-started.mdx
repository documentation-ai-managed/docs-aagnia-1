## Overview

Get started with Aagnia Technologies by following this quickstart guide. You will contact the sales team, schedule a consultation, scope your project, and complete onboarding essentials. Aagnia specializes in custom software development, enterprise web and mobile apps, industrial IoT, and AI-driven solutions. This process typically takes 1-2 weeks, depending on your project's complexity.

<Callout kind="info">
Before beginning, prepare:
- Your project requirements document
- Business goals and timeline
- Technical stack preferences (e.g., React for web, Flutter for mobile)
- Budget estimates
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Ensure you have the following ready:

- A clear project brief outlining your needs, such as web app development or IoT integration.
- Access to email and a scheduling tool like Google Calendar.
- Decision-makers available for consultations.

## Step-by-Step Onboarding Process

Follow these sequential steps to kick off your project with Aagnia.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact the Sales Team" icon="mail">
    Reach out via email or the contact form on the Aagnia website.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Email" icon="send">
        Send an email to `sales@aagnia.com` with your project overview.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Contact Form" icon="form">
        Visit `https://aagnia.com/contact` and submit the form.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    <CodeGroup tabs="Email Template,API Contact">
    ````html
    <form action="https://api.aagnia.com/v1/contact" method="POST">
      <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="your@email.com" required>
      <textarea name="message" placeholder="Describe your project..."></textarea>
      <button type="submit">Submit</button>
    </form>
    ````
    ````javascript
    fetch('https://api.aagnia.com/v1/contact', {
      method: 'POST',
      headers: { 'Content-Type': 'application/json' },
      body: JSON.stringify({
        email: 'your@email.com',
        message: 'Need custom IoT solution for manufacturing.'
      })
    });
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule Consultation" icon="calendar">
    A sales representative will respond within 24 hours. Reply to schedule a 30-minute call using the provided Calendly link.

    Review the agenda:
    - Discuss your business challenges
    - Outline technical requirements
    - Estimate timelines and costs
  </Step>

  <Step title="Project Scoping" icon="search">
    During the call, collaborate on a project scope document. This includes:

    | Phase       | Deliverables                  | Timeline |
    |-------------|-------------------------------|----------|
    | Discovery   | Requirements gathering       | 1 week  |
    | Design      | Architecture & UI/UX         | 2 weeks |
    | Development | Core features implementation | 4-8 weeks |
    | Testing     | QA & deployment              | 2 weeks |

    Sign the Statement of Work (SOW) to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Onboarding Essentials" icon="check-circle">
    Receive your project portal access at `https://dashboard.aagnia.com`.

    <CodeGroup tabs="Node.js,Python">
    ````javascript
    // Set up API client
    const AagniaAPI = require('aagnia-sdk');
    const client = new AagniaAPI({ apiKey: 'YOUR_API_KEY' });
    const projects = await client.projects.list();
    console.log(projects);
    ````
    ````python
    # Install: pip install aagnia-sdk
    from aagnia import Client
    client = Client(api_key='YOUR_API_KEY')
    projects = client.projects.list()
    print(projects)
    ````
    </CodeGroup>

    Join the Slack workspace and review the kickoff deck.
  </Step>
</Steps>
